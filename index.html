Motivation

We wanted to do a final assignment about something we were both interested in and knew alot about so we decided to to an analysis about the hit TV show, the Office. This gave us a lot of data to work with. Character pages, to make a network, and quotes and transcripts for text and sentiment analysis.

We were interested to discover something new about the show that may not be clear from watching it. Below we will share our findings. Enjoy!

Accident types and different weather conditions

Alright! Now we have interactive plots for comparing weather conditions and accident types.
Rush hour 16-18.
For all weather conditions we see that there is a significant
increase in accidents around rush hour, for all accident types.
Rush hour 7-9
When looking at the freezing tab we see a spike in accidents
that also occur in the morning rush hour as well.
When there is frost the driving conditions are harder to
manage and time of the day matter a bit less.
Move between frost and no frost tabs to see this!
